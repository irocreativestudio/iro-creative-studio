What We Found

You named your competitors.
We went deeper.

Koparo, Born Good, Beco, these are strong brands. Funded, scaled, respected. But none of them own what TrueBioWash owns: skin-first, fruit-forward, whole-family safe in a single laundry brand.

Koparo is broad home care, laundry is one of twenty products. Born Good is sophisticated and earthy, Brazilian Rosewood, Japanese Cypress. No fruit, no baby line. Beco is planet-first, bamboo, sustainability, $14M funded. Skin isn't their story.

MeeMee and LuvLap own the baby detergent shelf, but they look like every other baby brand. No eco angle, no premium positioning, no adult crossover. They're mass. You're not.

Nobody in India has claimed your combination. That's not a gap, that's a lane. A wide open lane.
